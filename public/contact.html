Frequently Asked Questions

Before contacting us, you might find your answer in our FAQ section. Here are some of the most common questions we receive:

How do I convert a PDF to Word?

Simply navigate to our PDF to Word converter, upload your PDF file, and click the convert button. Your editable Word document will be ready for download in seconds. Our converter maintains formatting, images, and text layout as closely as possible to the original document.

Is Convertlyyy free to use?

Yes! Convertlyyy is completely free for all users. We support our service through non-intrusive advertising, which allows us to provide high-quality conversion tools at no cost to you. There are no hidden fees, no premium tiers, and no limits on the number of conversions you can perform.

What file formats do you support?

We support a wide range of file formats including PDF, DOCX, DOC, XLSX, XLS, CSV, JPG, PNG, and more. Our tools can convert between these formats in various combinations, making document management simple and efficient for any workflow.

Are my files secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard SSL encryption for all file transfers, and your files are automatically deleted from our servers within 2 hours of processing. We never share, sell, or view your files. Your privacy and security are our top priorities.

Why did my conversion fail?

Conversion failures can occur for several reasons: the file might be password-protected, corrupted, exceed our size limits (50MB), or contain unsupported elements. Try removing password protection, reducing file size, or using a different source file. If problems persist, contact our support team with details about the error.
