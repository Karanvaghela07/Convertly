## Part 4: Indexing Fix - Step by Step

### Why "Discovered - Currently Not Indexed" Happens:
- Google found your pages but hasn't crawled/indexed them yet
- Can indicate low perceived quality or crawl budget issues
- Often affects new sites or sites with thin content

### Step-by-Step Fix:

#### Step 1: Submit Updated Sitemap
1. Go to Google Search Console
2. Navigate to Sitemaps
3. Remove old sitemap if present
4. Submit new sitemap: `https://convertlyyy.netlify.app/sitemap.xml`

#### Step 2: Request Indexing for Priority Pages
1. In Search Console, go to URL Inspection
2. Enter each important URL one by one:
   - `https://convertlyyy.netlify.app/`
   - `https://convertlyyy.netlify.app/pdf-to-word.html`
   - `https://convertlyyy.netlify.app/word-to-pdf.html`
   - `https://convertlyyy.netlify.app/merge-pdf.html`
3. Click "Request Indexing" for each

#### Step 3: Improve Crawl Signals
- Ping sitemap: `https://www.google.com/ping?sitemap=https://convertlyyy.netlify.app/sitemap.xml`
- Share links on social media (creates external signals)
- Post on relevant forums/communities

#### Step 4: Monitor Progress
- Check Search Console daily for first 2 weeks
- Watch "Coverage" report for status changes
- Pages typically move from "Discovered" to "Crawled" within 2-7 days
